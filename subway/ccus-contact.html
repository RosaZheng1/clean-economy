You can
Mail your request to the CRA

Before you mail your request, please read the following as it includes important information about limitations on rulings, circumstances where a ruling will not be issued, and processing requests:

  • Interpretations (paragraphs 11 and 12)
  • Rulings (paragraphs 15 to 19, and 22 to 31)
  • Processing requests for rulings and interpretations (paragraphs 35 to 46)

Send your request to:

GST/HST Rulings Directorate
Canada Revenue Agency
Place de Ville Tower A 5th floor
320 Queen St
Ottawa ON K1A 0L5
Canada

  • Interpretations (paragraphs 11 and 12)
